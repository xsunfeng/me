Movers: Exploring Refugee Movement with Social Media Data
Spring 2016 - Present
Movers is an exploratory visual analytics tool. we designed Movers to investigate the feasibility of using geo-referenced social media for understanding refugee movement. This system is designed to support analysts in systematically exploring geo-referenced twitter data in order to identify probably refugees. Identifying individual Syrian refugees who fled to Germany is used as case study to test the functionality of this tool.
Mark Simpson, Ying Xu, Feng Sun, Yanan Xin, Fridolin Linder and Xi Liu, "Movers: Exploring Refugee Movement with Social Media Data." (SpatialVA2016, a workshop at the GIScience 2016 conference)
